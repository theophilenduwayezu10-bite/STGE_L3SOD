welcome to Classroom Rules

1. Clear Expectations

Students must understand rules clearly: respect, punctuality, and participation.

2. Smart Procedures

Follow simple classroom routines like raising hand, submitting assignments on time.

3. Positive Reinforcement

Reward good behavior using praise, points, or small rewards.

4. Logical Consequences

Misbehavior leads to fair consequences like warning, extra tasks, or discussion.

5. Planned Responses

Teachers prepare how to react calmly to disruptions or challenges.
